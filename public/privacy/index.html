Synq Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Scope and Contact

This Privacy Policy describes how Synq ("Synq," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal information when you use the app.

Questions, privacy requests, or complaints can be sent to synqapp@gmail.com.

2. Personal Information We Collect

We collect the categories of personal information below, depending on which features you use.

  • Identifiers and account credentials: email address and password if you sign up with email; phone number, verification-related data, and reCAPTCHA-based verification flow if you sign up with phone; and your authentication user ID.
  • Profile and account content: display name, first name, last name, optional profile photo, interests, city, state, optional saved coordinates, status, memo, invite code, timestamps, and plan or event details you add.
  • Social and relationship data: friends, friend requests, mutual-connection data, invite-link or invite-code attribution, Synq counts, and last-Synq timestamps.
  • Communications data: chat participants, participant names and images, message text, timestamps, read markers, last-message metadata, and message reactions.
  • Notification and device data: Expo push token, app or device-related metadata needed to send and deduplicate notifications, and limited technical information tied to app behavior.
  • Feedback and support information: if you use the feedback flow or email us, we may receive the message contents, the optional contact email you provide, and basic platform information included in the drafted email.
  • On-device data: AsyncStorage data such as social caches, pending invite information, and local Synq status stored on your device to make the app work faster and resume correctly.

3. Sources of Information

  • Directly from you when you create an account, build your profile, message, add plans, or contact us.
  • From your device when you enable permissions like notifications, photo-library access, or foreground location.
  • From other users when they send you friend requests, connect with you, or interact with you in chats and shared plans.
  • From service providers that help us run Synq, including Firebase, Expo, and Google.

4. How We Use Personal Information

  • Create, authenticate, and secure accounts.
  • Provide profile, friend, availability, plan, and messaging features.
  • Show your profile and activity to the users you interact with through Synq.
  • Send push notifications about messages, friend requests, accepted requests, plan interest, and friend activity.
  • Generate venue suggestions using your city/state and shared interests when you choose that feature.
  • Respond to feedback, support, and account requests.
  • Prevent abuse, enforce our terms, troubleshoot problems, and maintain app reliability and security.

5. Permissions, Location, and Sensitive Data

Synq does not continuously track your location in the background. When you choose to use current location, the app requests foreground location permission, reads your coordinates once, reverse geocodes them, and stores your city, state, and saved coordinates in your profile until you edit or remove them.

If you upload a profile photo, Synq requests photo-library access only when you choose that action. If you enable push notifications, Synq requests notification permission so alerts can be delivered.

We use this information only to provide the features you request, not for targeted advertising.

6. Messages, Notifications, and AI Suggestions

Messages are stored so chat participants can view them in Synq. We also process message contents and metadata to support delivery, unread state, reactions, and notification previews.

If you use the suggestion feature, Synq sends your selected category, your city/state, and shared interests to backend services that call Google Generative AI and Google Places APIs to return venue suggestions. Those suggestions may be incomplete or inaccurate.

7. How We Disclose Personal Information

  • To other users: profile details, city/state, availability, plans, friend requests, and messages are visible to the people you connect or interact with through Synq.
  • To service providers: Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Storage, and Cloud Functions; Expo notifications; Google services used for venue suggestions; and your email app/provider when you use the in-app feedback mail flow.
  • For legal, security, or compliance reasons: when reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, enforce our terms, or protect Synq, our users, or others.
  • In connection with a business transaction: if Synq is involved in a merger, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, subject to applicable law.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising based on the current app code.

8. Retention and Deletion

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate Synq, maintain your account, provide the features you use, resolve disputes, and meet legal, tax, accounting, or security obligations.

If you delete your account through the app, Synq is designed to delete your profile document, friends, friend requests, notification-lock records, chats, messages, and authentication account. Some information may remain temporarily in logs, backups, or service-provider systems for a limited period.

9. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing.

You can already take certain actions inside the app, including updating your profile, changing or removing saved location information, updating interests, controlling device permissions, and deleting your account.

To make a privacy request, email synqapp@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

10. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Performance of our contract with you to provide the app and its features.
  • Your consent for optional permissions and features, such as location, profile-photo access, and notifications.
  • Our legitimate interests in operating, securing, improving, and supporting Synq.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

11. Children

Synq is not intended for children under 16. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child who was not permitted to use the service, we may delete that information and disable the account.

12. International Transfers

Synq and the providers we use may process personal information in countries other than the one where you live. By using Synq, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations, subject to applicable law.

13. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and may also provide additional notice in the app.

15. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email synqapp@gmail.com.
